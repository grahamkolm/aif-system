AIF™ 60-Minute Tactical Setup

0–5 Minutes • Arrival Scan

Observe the water before any gear is deployed.

  • Wind observation
  • Surface activity
  • Water colour







5–20 Minutes • Water Intelligence

Use boat, camera or marker rod to understand the water structure.

  • Depth at target area
  • Bottom composition
  • Surface and bottom temperature










20–40 Minutes • Tactical Decision

Based on observations decide how the swim will be fished.

  • Bait selection
  • Hookbait choice
  • Feed approach







40–60 Minutes • Drop Preparation

Prepare rods and finalize the first drop.

  • Confirm target depth
  • Confirm patrol direction
  • Prepare first drop
